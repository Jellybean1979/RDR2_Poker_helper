Poker Helper Guide

Street: The stage of the hand (Preflop, Flop, Turn, River).

Bet to Call: The amount you must match to stay in the hand.

Planned Raise Size: The amount you want to raise to if you’re ahead.

Positions:

  • UTG/UTG+1: (UTG = Under the Gun) First to act, play tight.
  • MP: Middle seats, play a bit wider.
  • CO/Button: (CO = Cut-off, Button = dealer) Late positions, best spots, play more hands.
  • Small Blind/Big Blind: Forced bets, tough spots, play carefully.

Board Cards:

  • Flop: First 3 community cards. Most hands are decided here — if you hit a pair or a strong draw, keep going.
  • Turn: Fourth card. Strengthens or kills your draws. If you improve, bet; if not, be cautious.
  • River: Fifth and final card. Final showdown, no more drawing — you either have a made hand or you fold.

Poker shorthand terms:

  • s: same suit
  • o: off suit
  • AKs: Ace and King, same suit
  • JTo: Jack and Ten, off suits
  • 77: Pair of Sevens
  • 94o: Weak, unconnected, offsuit hand

Poker Advice & Strategy

AI Weakness #1 — They Fold Too Much to Raises
Raise preflop with almost anything. Continuation bet the flop. Most NPCs fold unless they hit.

AI Weakness #2 — They Overpay When They Hit
When you hit a strong hand, value bet big. NPCs call with top pair, draws, two pair, and weak holdings.

RDR2 Exploit Strategy Loop
Preflop:
• If nobody has raised yet → YOU raise (AI folds too often).
• If someone already raised → tighten up and only continue with strong hands.

Flop:
• If they check to you → make a continuation bet (they fold too much).
• If they bet into you → call the flop (AI rarely bluffs the flop and overpays later).

Turn:
• If you improve → bet big (they call with weak pairs and draws).
• If you miss and face pressure → fold (AI bets strong hands on turn).

River:
• Do not bluff (AI calls too often).
• If you have a strong hand → value bet big (they overpay with worse hands).

NPC Behavior Patterns
Tight = folds to pressure
Loose = calls too much
Aggressive = bluffs more
Passive = rarely bluffs
